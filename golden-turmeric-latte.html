Golden Turmeric Latte

A warming, anti-inflammatory drink perfect for any time of day

Watch How to Make It

Prep Time

5 minutes

Cook Time

5 minutes

Servings

1

Ingredients

  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp ginger powder
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • Pinch of black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp coconut oil (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat almond milk in a small saucepan over medium heat
  2. Add turmeric, cinnamon, ginger, and black pepper
  3. Whisk until well combined and heated through
  4. Remove from heat and stir in honey
  5. Add coconut oil if using and whisk until melted
  6. Pour into a mug and enjoy

Nutrition Information (per serving)

Calories

120

Protein

2g

Carbs

18g

Fat

4g
