Tofu Scramble

A protein-rich vegan alternative to scrambled eggs, packed with vegetables and flavorful spices.

Prep Time

10 minutes

Cook Time

15 minutes

Servings

2 people

Description

This Tofu Scramble is a delicious and nutritious vegan breakfast option. Made with crumbled tofu, saut√©ed vegetables, and aromatic spices, it's a satisfying way to start your day with plant-based protein.

Vegetarian Vegan Gluten-Free High Protein

Ingredients

  • 1 block (14 oz) firm tofu, drained and crumbled
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 2 cups spinach, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 2 tbsp nutritional yeast
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh herbs for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Press and drain the tofu to remove excess water.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add onions and cook until translucent, about 3-4 minutes.
  4. Add garlic and cook for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  5. Add bell peppers and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Crumble the tofu into the skillet and add turmeric, cumin, and paprika.
  7. Cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  8. Add spinach and cook until wilted.
  9. Stir in nutritional yeast and season with salt and pepper.
  10. Garnish with fresh herbs if desired and serve hot.

Tips for Perfect Tofu Scramble

  • Press tofu well to remove excess water for better texture.
  • Use firm or extra-firm tofu for the best results.
  • Don't overcook the vegetables to maintain their crunch.
  • Add nutritional yeast for a cheesy flavor.
  • Serve with toast or wrap in a tortilla for a complete meal.
