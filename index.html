Our Mission

Urban communities are seeking verified organic food as awareness of health risks from pesticides grows. Yet, credible sources for organic produce are scarce. Rural farmers can grow organic, but often don't due to low prices and lack of direct market access. PlatePilot bridges this gap empowering farmers to sell organic fruits and veggies at double the rate of conventional produce, with quality checks by AI and professional inspectors. Join us in fueling a healthy, fair, and sustainable food future.
