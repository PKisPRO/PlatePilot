Chickpea Curry

A hearty and flavorful curry made with protein-rich chickpeas, aromatic spices, and creamy coconut milk.

Prep Time

15 minutes

Cook Time

30 minutes

Servings

4 people

Description

This Chickpea Curry is a delicious and nutritious dish that combines tender chickpeas with a rich, spiced tomato-coconut sauce. It's perfect for a cozy dinner and can be served with rice or naan bread.

Vegetarian Vegan Gluten-Free High Protein

Ingredients

  • 2 cans (15 oz each) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp coriander
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper (optional)
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add onion and cook until translucent, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add garlic and ginger, cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Add curry powder, turmeric, cumin, and coriander. Stir for 30 seconds.
  5. Add tomatoes and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  6. Add chickpeas and coconut milk. Bring to a simmer.
  7. Cook for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until sauce thickens.
  8. Season with salt and cayenne pepper if desired.
  9. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with rice or naan.

Nutritional Information

Calories

380

Protein

15g

Carbs

45g

Fat

18g

Tips for Perfect Chickpea Curry

  • Rinse chickpeas thoroughly to remove excess sodium.
  • Toast the spices briefly to enhance their flavor.
  • Use full-fat coconut milk for a creamier texture.
  • Let the curry simmer to develop deeper flavors.
  • Adjust spice levels according to your preference.
