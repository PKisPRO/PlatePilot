Mediterranean Salad

Salad Prep Time: 15 mins Servings: 4

A refreshing and healthy Mediterranean salad that combines fresh vegetables, olives, and feta cheese with a simple olive oil dressing.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mixed salad greens
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the salad greens, cucumber, cherry tomatoes, and red onion.
  2. Add the Kalamata olives and feta cheese.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, red wine vinegar, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  4. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy!
