Vegetable Soup

A hearty and nutritious soup packed with seasonal vegetables and aromatic herbs.

Prep Time

15 minutes

Cook Time

30 minutes

Servings

4 people

Description

This vegetable soup is a comforting and nutritious dish that combines a variety of fresh vegetables with aromatic herbs and spices. It's perfect for cold days or when you need a healthy, warming meal. The soup is naturally low in calories but high in vitamins and minerals.

Vegetarian Vegan Gluten-Free Low Calorie

Ingredients

  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 1 cup green beans, chopped
  • 1 cup corn kernels
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 6 cups vegetable broth
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add onions and garlic, saut√© until translucent.
  3. Add carrots and celery, cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Add zucchini and green beans, cook for 3 minutes.
  5. Pour in vegetable broth and diced tomatoes.
  6. Add thyme, oregano, and bay leaf.
  7. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes.
  8. Add corn kernels and cook for 5 more minutes.
  9. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  10. Remove bay leaf and garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

Nutritional Information

Calories

120

Protein

4g

Carbs

18g

Fat

5g

Tips for Perfect Vegetable Soup

  • Use fresh, seasonal vegetables for the best flavor.
  • Don't overcook the vegetables to maintain their texture.
  • Add herbs towards the end of cooking to preserve their flavor.
  • Let the soup rest for 10 minutes before serving to allow flavors to meld.
  • Serve with crusty bread for a complete meal.
