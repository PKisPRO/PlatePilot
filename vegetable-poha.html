Vegetable Poha

A light and nutritious breakfast dish made with flattened rice, fresh vegetables, and aromatic spices.

Prep Time

10 minutes

Cook Time

15 minutes

Servings

2 people

Description

Vegetable Poha is a popular Indian breakfast dish that combines flattened rice with a medley of fresh vegetables and aromatic spices. It's light, nutritious, and perfect for starting your day.

Vegetarian Vegan Gluten-Free Low Calorie

Ingredients

  • 2 cups thick poha (flattened rice)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 potato, diced
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1/2 cup green peas
  • 2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 8-10 curry leaves
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish
  • Lemon juice (optional)

Instructions

  1. Rinse the poha in water and drain. Let it sit for 5 minutes to soften.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds and cumin seeds.
  3. When the seeds crackle, add curry leaves and green chilies.
  4. Add onions and saut√© until translucent.
  5. Add potatoes and carrots. Cook for 5 minutes until slightly tender.
  6. Add green peas and cook for 2 minutes.
  7. Add turmeric powder and salt. Mix well.
  8. Add the softened poha and gently mix everything together.
  9. Cook for 3-4 minutes on low heat, stirring occasionally.
  10. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with lemon wedges.

Nutritional Information

Tips for Perfect Vegetable Poha

  • Use thick poha for better texture and less mushiness.
  • Don't soak the poha for too long to prevent it from becoming mushy.
  • Cook vegetables until they are slightly tender but still have a crunch.
  • Add a squeeze of lemon juice for a tangy flavor.
  • Serve hot for the best taste and texture.
