Quinoa Bowl

A nutritious and colorful bowl packed with protein-rich quinoa, fresh vegetables, and a zesty dressing.

Prep Time

15 minutes

Cook Time

20 minutes

Servings

2 people

Description

This Quinoa Bowl is a perfect balance of protein, fiber, and essential nutrients. It combines fluffy quinoa with crisp vegetables, creamy avocado, and a tangy lemon-tahini dressing for a satisfying and healthy meal.

Vegetarian Vegan Gluten-Free High Protein

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed
  • 2 cups water or vegetable broth
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp tahini
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Rinse quinoa thoroughly under cold water.
  2. In a medium saucepan, combine quinoa and water/broth. Bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes until water is absorbed.
  4. Remove from heat and let stand for 5 minutes. Fluff with a fork.
  5. While quinoa cooks, prepare the vegetables.
  6. Make the dressing by whisking together olive oil, tahini, lemon juice, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  7. In a large bowl, combine cooked quinoa, vegetables, and parsley.
  8. Drizzle with dressing and toss gently to combine.
  9. Top with avocado slices and serve immediately.

Nutritional Information

Calories

420

Protein

18g

Tips for Perfect Quinoa Bowl

  • Rinse quinoa well to remove any bitter coating.
  • Use vegetable broth instead of water for more flavor.
  • Let quinoa cool slightly before adding vegetables to prevent wilting.
  • Add avocado just before serving to prevent browning.
  • Adjust the dressing ingredients to your taste preference.
