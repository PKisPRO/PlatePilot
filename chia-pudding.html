Chia Pudding

A nutritious and delicious breakfast or snack packed with omega-3s and fiber

Prep Time

5 minutes

Rest Time

4 hours or overnight

Calories

250 per serving

Instructions

  2. 2

  3. 3

    Let the mixture sit for 5 minutes, then stir again to prevent clumping.

  4. 4

  6. 6

    Top with fresh berries and optional nuts or granola.
