Vegetable Stir-Fry

A quick and healthy stir-fry loaded with colorful vegetables and aromatic Asian flavors.

Description

This Vegetable Stir-Fry is a vibrant and nutritious dish that combines fresh vegetables with a savory sauce. It's quick to prepare, making it perfect for busy weeknights, and can be customized with your favorite vegetables and protein sources.

Vegetarian Vegan Gluten-Free

Ingredients

  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup vegetable broth
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the sauce by mixing soy sauce, cornstarch, and vegetable broth in a small bowl.
  2. Heat cooking oil in a large wok or skillet over high heat.
  3. Add garlic and ginger, stir for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  4. Add carrots and broccoli, stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Add bell pepper, snap peas, and mushrooms. Continue stir-frying for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Pour the sauce over the vegetables and stir well.
  7. Cook for 1-2 minutes until the sauce thickens.
  8. Drizzle with sesame oil and season with salt and pepper.
  9. Serve hot over rice or noodles.

Tips for Perfect Vegetable Stir-Fry

  • Cut all vegetables into similar sizes for even cooking.
  • Have all ingredients prepared before starting to cook.
  • Keep the heat high and stir constantly for the best results.
  • Add vegetables in order of cooking time (hardest to softest).
  • Don't overcrowd the pan to ensure proper stir-frying.
